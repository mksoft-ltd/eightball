**Last Updated: November 28, 2024**

## Introduction

FroggyEye ("we", "our", or "us") operates the 8 Ball Wisdom mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use its core functionality.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (for advertising purposes)

**Usage Data**
- App interaction data (such as feature usage)
- Crash reports and performance data

### Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

**Google AdMob**
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Usage data
- Advertising ID

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Google Play In-App Purchases**
If you make a purchase to remove ads, Google Play handles the transaction. We do not collect or store your payment information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Display relevant advertisements
- Process in-app purchases
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze app usage to fix bugs and improve features

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Advertising Partners**: To serve ads (Google AdMob)
- **Analytics Providers**: To understand app usage
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law

## Data Retention

We retain automatically collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

## Your Choices

**Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**
You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

**Remove Ads**
You can purchase a lifetime subscription ($0.99) to remove all advertisements from the App.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.
