# Privacy Policy for 8 Ball Wisdom

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

## Introduction

FroggyEye ("we", "our", or "us") operates the 8 Ball Wisdom mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use its core functionality.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (for advertising purposes)

**Usage Data**
- App interaction data (such as feature usage)
- Crash reports and performance data

### Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

**Google AdMob**
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Usage data
- Advertising ID

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Google Play & Apple App Store In-App Purchases**
If you make a purchase to remove ads, Google Play (on Android) or Apple App Store (on iOS) handles the transaction. We do not collect or store your payment information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Display relevant advertisements
- Process in-app purchases
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze app usage to fix bugs and improve features

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Advertising Partners**: To serve ads (Google AdMob)
- **Analytics Providers**: To understand app usage
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law

## Data Retention

We retain automatically collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

## Your Choices

**Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**
You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

**Remove Ads**
You can make a one-time in-app purchase to remove all advertisements from the App. The current price is shown in the app's purchase dialog and is set by the App Store / Play Store listing.

## Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)
If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time via your device settings
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, email **privacy@froggyeye.com**.

## Your Rights (CCPA — California)
California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. The App does not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, email **privacy@froggyeye.com**.

## Data Deletion
Because the App does not require an account and stores only local preferences on your device, you can delete all local data by uninstalling the App. For any data held by our ad partners, use the opt-out controls described above or contact us at **privacy@froggyeye.com**.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.
